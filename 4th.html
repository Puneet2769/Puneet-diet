4th Day Diet Plan

Diet Plan

For Vegetarians:

BREAKFAST:
ONE BROWN BREAD SANDWICH (NO POTATO)

MID MORNING:
LEMON WATER + CHIA SEEDS

LUNCH:
ONE JOWAR ROTI + GREEN SABZI + DAL + SALAD

EVE:
CLEAR SOUP

DINNER:
ONLY SABZI IN GOOD QUANTITY (NO POTATO)

BEFORE BED:
WARM WATER

For Non-Vegetarians:

BREAKFAST:
2 APPLES

MID MORNING:
COCONUT WATER

LUNCH:
2 EGG WHITE OMELETTE + GREEN SABZI (NO POTATO)

EVE:
ROASTED CHANA (ONE SMALL BOWL)

DINNER:
ONE JOWAR ROTI + GREEN SABZI

BEFORE BED:
WARM WATER
