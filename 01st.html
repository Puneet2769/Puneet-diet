1st Day Diet Plan

Diet Plan

BREAKFAST:
2 wheat chapati (small) + any sabzi (no potato)

MID-MORNING:
Warm water (empty stomach) + lemon (200ml), 1 apple

LUNCH:
1 besan chilla

EVE:
Roasted chana, green tea

DINNER:
Any sabzi + curd

NOTES:
– Water intake: Your weight × 2 = number of 200ml glasses daily
– 1 glass warm water + lemon one hour before dinner
– Strictly avoid: poha, upma, idli, oats, packed items, deep-fried food, dosa, sabudana, cornflakes, daliya, khichdi, namira, sugar, potato, papad
